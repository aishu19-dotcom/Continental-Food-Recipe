How to Make Panko Crusted Cottage Cheese:

  1. Marinate the paneer with some salt, oregano, smoked paprika and cumin powder.
  2. In a bowl make a batter with corn flour, white flour, milk and salt.
  3. This will help the breadcrumbs cling to the paneer.
  4. Dust some white flour over the paneer as well.
  5. Coat with the batter and breadcrumbs.
  6. Fry the paneer in a mixture of butter and olive oil.
